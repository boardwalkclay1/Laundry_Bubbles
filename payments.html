Payments & payouts

Connect secure payouts and add your payout handles. Cards and bank details are handled by Stripe, not this app.

Stripe Connect payouts

Stripe securely handles card details, bank accounts, identity checks, and compliance. We never see or store card or bank numbers in this app.

You’ll be redirected to Stripe to complete onboarding or update your payout details. When you’re done, you’ll be sent back to Laundry Bubbles.
